Who likes free stuff?

We are teaming up with Gear Talk and Gear Guts to give away a custom 2x12 guitar cabinet! This giveaway is pretty simple - design YOUR custom cabinet in the Customizer below, take a screen shot of the cabinet, and share it on your social media pages (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook) with the hashtag #CustomCabGiveaway and tag the three of us in the post. That's it. There are no limits on the number of entries you can submit as long as you abide by the previously mentioned rules. One lucky person will win the cabinet that they designed and we'll give it to them. How rad is that? Very.

You can find us on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram by searching "New Vintage Amps."

RULES

  1. Follow New Vintage Amps, Gear Talk, and Gear Guts
  2. Design your cabinet using the Customizer and options below.
  3. Take a screen shot of the cabinet
  4. Share it on your social media pages (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook) with the hashtag #CustomCabGiveaway
  5. Tag New Vintage Amps, Gear Talk, and Gear Guts

FINE PRINT

The winner will be randomly selected on February 22, 2017. We will notify the winner via the social media outlet upon which their winning submission was posted, so PLEASE follow us on Twitter and Instagram (@newvintageamps) and "like" us on Facebook. We will cover shipping to the lower 48 States. All other shipping will be sorted out between us and the winner. The winner will be contacted about speaker selection for the cabinet.
