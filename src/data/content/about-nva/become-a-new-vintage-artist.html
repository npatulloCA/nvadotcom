When you become a New Vintage Amplifiers artist, you get a lot more than just a great amp. One of the things that we pride ourselves on, and one of the reasons we have such a great artist roster, is our customer service. We understand that every customer is just as valuable as the last, and we strive to provide excellence in everything we do. From building the most reliable, tour-worthy amplifiers, answering emails, or helping out when problems arise on the road, we know the value of great service. All too often we hear of musicians that can't get help from other gear companies, pieces that go down on every tour, and a lack of transparency between the builder and the artist. We have an incredible relationship with all of our artists that goes beyond the gear that they're playing. It's a great thing to know that any need you may have, big or small, will be taken care of and addressed quickly.

For touring artists, producers, engineers, and session players interested in becoming a part of the New Vintage Amplifiers family of artists, we are glad to extend our very competitive artist pricing structure and world-class support. To request more information regarding our artist program, please email an electronic press kit and information about yourself, your band, or your studio, such as record sales, past and upcoming tours, YouTube hits, albums you've played on, artists you've worked with, and anything else you may feel we need to know to info@newvintageamps.com.

Please note that we do not offer free product or paid sponsorship deals to any of our artists.
