Where classic American and British tones meet

  The Retribution 30 is our combination of tweed and British tones. Naturally a little darker, the Retribution 30 is designed to produce a tone that is British-inspired but without the squawky, brittle tone. The Retribution 30 features a unique Drive switch that, when off, has a round, warm clean to classic EL84 crunch. Flip the Drive switch on and it moves the midrange point for a more aggressive tone as well as increased gain and touch sensitivity. This amplifier is known for its unique and desirable distortion characteristic. The Retribution 30 is perfect for a player that is looking for a straight-forward, no frills amp.

Technical Specifications
