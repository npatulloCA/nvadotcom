Rooted in heart of classic american tone

The SA-45 and lower-wattage SA-25 are two amps that nail the classic American Country, Blues, and Rock tones. The SA Series of amplifiers have a nice round, warm tone that can bite when needed. The drive switch introduces more midrange growl, changing from clean to overdriven tones depending largely on the player's touch. These amps are designed to be able to sing or howl depending on how you play. This is a rare example of an amp that is influenced by the player. The SA series of amps really shine when played by a dynamic guitarist. The balanced tone provides the ideal foundation for pedals and effects.

Technical specifications for the SA-45 and SA-25

SA-45

  • 45 Watts of Power
  • Tube Configuration: 5AR4, 2-EL34 or 2-6L6, 3-12AX7
  • Controls Include: Volume, Gain, Drive, Treble, Mid, Bass, Brightness
  • Model Can Be Easily Biased to Use EL34's or 6L6's
  • Drive Switch On Front Panel Changes Preamp Midrange Voicing and Gain
  • Impedance Switch for 4, 8, and 16 Ohm Cabinets With 2 Parallel Outs

SA-25

  • 25 Watts of Power
  • Tube Configuration: 5AR4, 2-6V6, 3-12AX7
  • Controls Include: Volume, Gain, Drive, Treble, Mid, Bass, Brightness
  • Drive Switch On Front Panel Changes Preamp Midrange Voicing and Gain
  • Impedance Switch for 4, 8, and 16 Ohm Cabinets With 2 Parallel Outs
