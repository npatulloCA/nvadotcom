Speaker Cable

The connection between your tube amplifier and speaker is the most important connection in your rig. Not all speaker cables are created equal. Our speaker cables are made out of rugged Canare cable and jumbo connectors to ensure durability that stands the test of time. We use a special kind of cable that acts much like a humbucker and reduces unwanted noise that many speaker cables add between your amplifier and speaker. Think of this as your last line of defense against noise.
