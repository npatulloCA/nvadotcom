A guitar speaker cabinet for every player

Our attention to detail in both design and construction sets New Vintage guitar speaker cabinets apart from the rest. Built in-house, our 1x12, 2x12, and 4x12 guitar cabinets are constructed with 15mm BB/BB grade, void-free Baltic Birch and all-metal hardware to ensure long-lasting durability and the best tone. All of our cabinets are designed to work with the speakers that we load them with. Thoughtful design, industry-leading materials, and careful craftsmanship give our cabinets a larger, tighter sound than others available on the market. Our 2x12 and 4x12 cabinets come standard with a Plug and Play switching system that allows you to select between 16 and 4 Ohm mono inputs or isolate the left and right set of speakers for stereo 8 Ohm inputs. Our cabinets come standard with your choice of Celestion's Classic Series line of speakers, but we can load it with any speaker from Celestion's AlNiCo or Heritage lines, as well as Weber, Eminence, WGS, Mojo, or Jensen speakers. Prices may vary for speakers outside of Celestion's Classic Series.
